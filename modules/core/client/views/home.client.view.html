What is FIRMS


FIRMS (Field Inspection Report Management System) was inspired from a coop experience at a structural engineering office, where the company needed an automated method of writing/storing field reports submitted by their field inspectors. The field inspector would go out to construction sites, inspect the quality of the work and ensure if the site is up to the engineerâ€™s standards and instructions.
